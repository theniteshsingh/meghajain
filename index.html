Ch. V Enquiries

Questions, answered plainly.

If your question isn't here, write in — every message is read personally.

Ask a question
01 Do I need to go to court, or can matters be settled out of court?

Many disputes can be resolved through negotiation, mediation or settlement — saving time, cost and stress. Where court is the right forum, the matter is prepared and represented fully.

02 Can you help with both personal and business legal matters?

Yes. The practice covers personal matters (criminal, family, property) alongside business work (corporate, contracts, compliance and commercial disputes).

03 How do you handle fee arrangements?

Fees are discussed clearly and in writing at the outset, scoped to the matter at hand. No surprises — you always know what you're paying for.

04 How long does it typically take to resolve a case?

It depends on forum, complexity and the other side's conduct. After an initial review, you'll get a candid timeline and a realistic view of what to expect.

05 What should I bring to my initial consultation?

Any documents, notices, agreements or correspondence relevant to your matter — plus a clear summary of what's happened. The more context, the sharper the advice.
